Welcome to Emotion Match game!

Humans are complex beings, and reading their emotions are not always easy for everyone. But we can always get more familiar with some practice.

In this game you get to match the name of several emotions to their corresponding facial expressions.

Your goal:

Match the emotion name with the right image

Instructions

  1. You will be given four (4) images depicting different emotional reaction and facial expressions and the name of an emotion
  2. You'll click on the image the best represent the given emotion
  3. For every correct answer, one point will be added to your score board
  4. For every incorrect answer, one point will be added to your incorrect answer tally
  5. After 5 rounds, you'll get your final score

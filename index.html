Welcome to Emotion Match game!

Humans are complex beings, and reading their emotions are not always easy for everyone. But we can always get more familiar with some practice.

In this game you get to match the name of several emotions to their corresponding facial expressions.

Your goal:

Match the emotion name with the right image within the time limit

Instructions

  1. You will be given four (4) images depicting different emotional reactions and facial expressions and the name of an emotion
  2. Click on the image the best represent the given emotion
  3. One point will be added to your score for every second left on the counter when you click the correct image
  4. Incorrect guesses sadly won't earn points
  5. If no image is clicked within 10 seconds, a new round will begin automatically
  6. For every incorrect answer, one point will be added to your incorrect answer tally
  7. After 6 rounds, you will receive your final score
